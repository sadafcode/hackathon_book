# Humanoid Robot Development

This chapter covers the specific aspects of developing humanoid robots, including kinematics, dynamics, bipedal locomotion, balance control, and manipulation. It focuses on the engineering challenges and solutions for creating robots capable of natural human interactions.

## Weekly Breakdown (Weeks 11-12): Humanoid Robot Development
- Humanoid robot kinematics and dynamics
- Bipedal locomotion and balance control
- Manipulation and grasping with humanoid hands
- Natural human-robot interaction design
